# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-06-26

FinTrack Pro ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## TL;DR

**We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal or financial data. Everything stays on your device.**

## Information We Store Locally (on your device only)

The following data is stored **locally on your device** using encrypted storage:

- Account information (name, email, hashed password)
- Financial records (income, expenses, goals, budgets, transactions)
- Photos you attach to transactions (stored locally)
- App preferences (theme, currency, language)
- Achievement progress and streaks

## Information We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ No analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No crash reporting to external servers
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No location data
- ❌ No contacts, calendar, or other personal data
- ❌ No financial data transmitted off-device

## Camera & Photos

FinTrack Pro requests access to your camera and photo library **only** to:
- Scan receipts using OCR (processed on-device)
- Attach images to transactions
- Set your profile avatar

These photos are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded.

## Biometric Authentication

If you enable Face ID / Touch ID / Fingerprint unlock, the app uses your device's secure authentication system. We do not have access to your biometric data — it never leaves your device's secure enclave.

## Notifications

The app can send local notifications (budget alerts, savings reminders, monthly summaries). These are scheduled on-device and do not require any server connection.

## Data Export & Backup

You can export your data at any time via **Settings → Data → Export as CSV** or **Export as JSON**. This exports a file that you choose where to save (locally or to a service of your choice).

## Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data via **Settings → Account → Delete account**. This action is irreversible and removes everything from your device.

## Children's Privacy

FinTrack Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
