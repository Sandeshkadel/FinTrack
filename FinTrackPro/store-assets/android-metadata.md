## Full Description

**FinTrack Pro** is a premium, offline-first personal finance tracker designed to help you take control of your money. Beautiful charts, smart insights, OCR receipt scanning, savings goals, and budgets — all stored securely on your device.

No subscriptions. No ads. No tracking. Just a great finance app.

### ✨ FEATURES

🔒 **100% PRIVATE & OFFLINE** — Your data never leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

📊 **STUNNING CHARTS** — Beautifully animated line, donut, bar, and progress-ring charts.

📸 **OCR RECEIPT SCANNING** — Snap a receipt; we'll extract amount, date, and merchant automatically.

💰 **MULTI-IMAGE RECORDS** — Attach up to 8 photos to any income or expense.

🏆 **SAVINGS GOALS** — Custom icons and colors. Smooth animations. Confetti rewards.

📈 **SMART BUDGETS** — Weekly / monthly / yearly limits with auto-calculated usage bars.

📅 **CALENDAR VIEW** — Monthly grid with daily drill-down.

📑 **DETAILED REPORTS** — Daily / weekly / monthly / yearly breakdowns with CSV / JSON export.

💡 **AI-POWERED INSIGHTS** — Spending analysis, top categories, savings streaks.

🔍 **QUICK SEARCH** — Find any transaction instantly.

🎨 **BEAUTIFUL THEMES** — Light, Dark, or System. Glass-morphism design.
