## Description

**FinTrack Pro** is a premium, offline-first personal finance tracker designed to help you take control of your money. With a beautiful, modern interface inspired by Apple Wallet and Revolut, FinTrack Pro combines powerful features with the simplicity of a native iOS experience.

### ✨ WHY FINTRACK PRO?

🔒 **100% Private & Offline** — Your data never leaves your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Just you and your finances.

📊 **Stunning Charts** — Beautifully animated line, donut, bar, and progress-ring charts that make your data come alive.

📸 **OCR Receipt Scanning** — Snap a photo of any receipt and we'll extract the amount, date, and merchant automatically.

💰 **Multi-Image Records** — Attach up to 8 photos to any income or expense for complete documentation.

🏆 **Savings Goals** — Set goals with custom icons and colors. Watch your progress with smooth animations and confetti rewards.

📈 **Smart Budgets** — Set weekly, monthly, or yearly limits with auto-calculated usage bars that warn you before you overspend.

📅 **Calendar View** — See your income and expenses on a beautiful monthly calendar with daily drill-down.

📑 **Detailed Reports** — Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly breakdowns with category breakdowns, savings rates, and CSV/JSON export.

💡 **AI-Powered Insights** — Get personalized analysis of your spending habits, top categories, savings streaks, and recommendations.

🔍 **Quick Search** — Find any transaction instantly with full-text search across titles, categories, and notes.

🎨 **Beautiful Themes** — Light, Dark, or System — with smooth transitions and glass-morphism design.
